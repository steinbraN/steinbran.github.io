sjRecorder

High-quality 60 fps video recording designed for sports, coaching, biomechanics, and detailed motion analysis.

Record video prepared for analysis

sjRecorder creates smooth, high-quality video for athlete analysis and professional sports workflows.

Features

  • High-quality 60 fps recording
  • Designed for fast sporting movement
  • User-selected storage through the Files interface
  • Simple record-and-save workflow
  • Local control of recordings
  • No account required

Privacy and file access

sjRecorder uses camera access only for recording video requested by the user. Recordings remain under the user’s control.
