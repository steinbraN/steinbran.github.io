sjRecorder

High-quality 60 fps video recording designed for sports, biomechanics, coaching, and detailed motion analysis.

Record video prepared for analysis

sjRecorder is designed to create smooth, high-quality video for athlete analysis and professional sports workflows.

Recordings can be saved to a location selected by the user through the iOS Files interface and later used with applications such as sjCropVideo and sjTrAnalyse.

High-quality recording

Record smooth video at 60 frames per second for detailed review of fast sporting movements.

Designed for sports

Created for ski jumping, coaching, biomechanics, motion analysis, research, and athlete development.

User-selected storage

Choose where recordings are saved using the standard iOS Files interface.

Private and local

Videos remain under the user’s control. sjRecorder does not upload recordings or collect personal data.
