sjCropVideo

Automatically track a moving subject, keep it centered, level the video, and export a clean result.

Automatic tracking and cropping for sports video

sjCropVideo follows a moving athlete with on-device object detection and adjusts the crop to keep the subject centered.

Features

  • Automatic subject tracking
  • Centered crop throughout the clip
  • Two-point video leveling
  • Horizontal flip
  • Tracking or manual clip selection
  • High-quality local export

Privacy and file access

sjCropVideo accesses only videos explicitly selected by the user. Tracking, cropping, leveling, and export happen on-device.
