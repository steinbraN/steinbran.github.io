sjCropVideo

Automatically track a moving subject, keep it centered, level the video, and export a professionally cropped result for sports analysis and coaching.

Automatic tracking for sports videos

sjCropVideo follows the selected athlete using on-device object detection and automatically keeps the subject centered while producing a new cropped video.

Originally developed for ski jumping, the app is equally useful for many sports where following a moving athlete makes analysis easier.

Automatic object tracking

Tracks the moving athlete and continuously adjusts the crop to keep the subject centered throughout the recording.

Video leveling

Correct tilted recordings by defining a reference line before exporting the final video.

Horizontal flip

Mirror videos when required, making recordings from opposite sides of a venue easy to compare.

High-quality export

Produces a stabilized cropped video suitable for coaching, sharing, and further analysis.

Local processing

Tracking, cropping, and export are performed entirely on the device using Apple Vision and Core ML. Videos are never uploaded.

Designed for sports

Ideal for ski jumping, athletics, coaching, biomechanics, and technical motion analysis.
