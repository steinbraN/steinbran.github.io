Automatic tracking and cropping for sports video

sjCropVideo follows a moving athlete with on-device object detection and adjusts the crop to keep the subject centered.

It is designed for sports workflows where a stable, analysis-ready video makes technical review easier.

Flexible clip selection

High-quality export

Produce a clean video for coaching and analysis.

Local processing
