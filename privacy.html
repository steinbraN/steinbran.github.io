Privacy Policy

Privacy information for applications developed by Steinar Braaten.

Summary

The applications covered by this policy do not collect, sell, transmit, or share personal information.

Video, image, CSV, and analysis files remain under the user’s control and are processed locally unless the user explicitly chooses to save, export, or share them.

Applications covered

This policy applies to applications including:

  • sjRecorder
  • sjTrAnalyse
  • sjCropVideo
  • sjVideoCrop
  • sjTracker
  • sjTrackerAnnotated

Information collection

These applications do not require user accounts and do not collect names, email addresses, contact details, device identifiers, location data, usage analytics, or advertising information.

The applications do not use third-party advertising or tracking technologies.

Camera access

Applications such as sjRecorder use the camera only to record video requested by the user.

Camera recordings are not uploaded to a server by the application. The user chooses where recordings are saved.

Files and folders

Some applications allow users to select files or folders through Apple’s standard file picker.

The applications access only files or folders explicitly selected by the user. This may include video files, CSV files, and exported analysis results.

Files are not accessed without user permission.

Local processing

Video processing, cropping, tracking, synchronization, graph generation, and biomechanics analysis are performed locally on the user’s device.

The applications do not automatically transmit video, motion data, or analysis results to Steinar Braaten or to an external service.

Data sharing and sale

Personal data is not sold, rented, shared, or provided to advertisers or data brokers.

Children’s privacy

The applications are tools for video recording, sports analysis, research, and coaching. They do not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if application functionality or legal requirements change. The latest version will always be available on this page.
