Privacy Policy

Privacy information for applications developed by Steinar Braaten.

Summary

The applications covered by this policy do not collect, sell, transmit, or share personal information.

Video, image, CSV, and analysis files remain under the user’s control and are processed locally unless the user explicitly chooses to save, export, or share them.

Applications covered

sjRecorder, sjCropVideo, sjTrAnalyse, sjTracker, and sjTrackerAnnotated.

Information collection

The apps do not require user accounts and do not collect names, contact details, device identifiers, location data, usage analytics, or advertising information.

Camera access

Apps such as sjRecorder use the camera only to record video requested by the user.

Files and local processing

The apps access only files or folders explicitly selected by the user. Video processing, cropping, tracking, synchronization, graphs, and analysis are performed locally.

Changes to this policy

The latest version will remain available on this page.
