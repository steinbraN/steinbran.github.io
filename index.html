sjTrAnalyse

sjTrAnalyse analyzes and compares synchronized ski jump videos using biomechanics data generated from AI pose estimation.

The app visualizes joint angles, balance, and rotation to support coaching, research, and athlete development.

macOS

sjCropVideo

sjCropVideo prepares, crops, levels, and exports video clips for sports and motion analysis workflows.

Coming soon

sjTracker

sjTracker will generate motion tracking data from ski jump videos using AI-based pose estimation.

Privacy Policy

sjTrAnalyse does not collect, transmit, store, or share personal information.

All video and CSV processing is performed locally on the user's Mac. No files are uploaded to any server.

The app does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies.
