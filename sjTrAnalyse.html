sjTrAnalyse

Compare two synchronized sports videos together with motion data, graphs, and exported analysis.

Video and biomechanics comparison in one view

sjTrAnalyse combines two synchronized videos with matching CSV motion data so performances can be reviewed side by side.

Features

  • Synchronized dual video
  • Automatic file pairing
  • Ankle, knee, hip, and balance graphs
  • Rotation score
  • Fixed or relative scales
  • HD analysis video export

Privacy and file access

Files are accessed only after the user explicitly selects them. Processing, graph generation, and export are performed locally on the Mac.
