sjTracker

AI pose tracking for ski jump analysis, with fast local processing and CSV export.

Automatic athlete tracking and body pose estimation

sjTracker detects the athlete throughout a ski jumping video, estimates body pose frame by frame, and exports joint coordinates for biomechanical analysis.

Features

  • Automatic athlete detection
  • AI body pose estimation
  • Batch processing of complete folders
  • CSV export of body joint coordinates
  • Fast processing optimized for Mac
  • Works offline with no account

Privacy and file access

Video and pose processing are performed locally on the Mac. Files are accessed only after the user selects a video or folder. No video or analysis data is uploaded.
